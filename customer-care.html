Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

How long does a typical project take?

Project timelines vary based on scope and complexity. Small projects typically take 2-4 weeks, while larger initiatives may take 8-12 weeks or more. We'll provide a detailed timeline and milestones during your initial consultation.

What is your revision policy?

We include unlimited revisions during the development phase to ensure complete satisfaction. After launch, we provide a 30-day revision window. Additional revisions after this period are available as part of our support packages.

Do you offer post-launch support?

Yes! We offer comprehensive maintenance and support packages after launch. These include bug fixes, performance monitoring, security updates, and general maintenance to keep your solution running smoothly.

How can I track my project progress?

You'll have access to a dedicated project dashboard where you can view real-time updates, milestones, and deliverables. Your project manager will also provide regular weekly updates via email and schedule check-in calls as needed.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards, bank transfers, and wire payments. For enterprise clients, we offer flexible payment plans and invoicing options. Please discuss payment terms with your account manager.
